About TantraFesta 2023
Tantrafiesta is the annual science and technology festival of the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Nagpur. Tantrafiesta hosts a plethora of social initiatives and outreach programs such as hackathons, coding contests, robo-races, debates, and seminars that help inspire the next generation of scientists and engineers. Tantrafiesta aims to provide a platform in order to establish a forum that will enable the exchange of knowledge, experience, and information related to a range of emerging technologies. This year, TantaFiesta is focusing on the use of greener technologies, and how utilizing greener technologies is the way forward.
